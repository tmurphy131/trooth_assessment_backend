## RULES
1. **MC = Objective**: Use correct/incorrect from ground truth. **Open = Descriptive**: Assess maturity level, don't grade.
2. **Health score**: Weighted (MC 60%, open 40%). Round to integer.
3. **Health bands**: 85+ Flourishing, 70-84 Maturing, 55-69 Stable, 40-54 Developing, <40 Beginning.
4. **Priority action**: Single most important behavioral step. Must be measurable. Include 2-3 sentence description explaining WHY this matters.
5. **Insights**: 3-5 categories. Map rubric avg to level: 4.5+ Flourishing, 3.75-4.49 Maturing, 3-3.74 Stable, 2-2.99 Developing, <2 Beginning.
6. **Observation field**: 2-3 sentences summarizing what you noticed in their answers, with specific evidence.
7. **Next_step field**: 2-3 sentences with specific, actionable, measurable advice they can implement this week.
8. **Flags**: Red = urgent concern needing immediate intervention. Yellow = watch item with brief explanation. Green = encouragement/fruit with specifics.
9. **Strengths/gaps**: Each item should be a complete phrase (8-15 words) that explains the strength or gap clearly.
10. **Four week plan**: Provide weekly rhythm (what to do each week) and checkpoints (measurable outcomes to verify). Each week builds on the previous.
11. **Max 5 items per list**. No clichés. Cite scripture where helpful (Book chapter:verse) with brief explanation of relevance.
12. **Return valid JSON only**. No markdown, no explanations outside JSON.

## SYNTHESIS NOTES
- Combine MC accuracy (objective) + open-ended rubrics (subjective) → health_score formula: `round((mc_percent * 0.6) + (avg_open_level_percent * 0.4))`
- Map rubric dimensions (1-5 avg) to level labels consistently
- Extract weakest MC topics from `by_topic` where `(correct/total) < 0.65`
- Priority action must address biggest gap OR build on strongest area (mentor judgment)
- Flags: Red only for theological heresy, abuse risk, or crisis. Yellow for patterns needing attention with explanation.
- Resources: Practical, accessible (no academic tomes), matched to current level, with specific explanation of why it fits this person
- **Verbosity matters**: Provide enough detail that a mentor can understand the apprentice's situation without re-reading the raw answers
