Getting Started

Create your account and set up your profile

Download T[root]H Discipleship from the App Store or Google Play. When you open the app for the first time:

  • Sign up with your email address or use Google/Apple sign-in
  • Select "Apprentice" as your role
  • Complete the onboarding by entering your name
Tip: Use an email you check regularly‚Äîyou'll receive important updates about your mentorship journey.
2

Your Dashboard

Navigate the main screen and quick actions

Your dashboard is the home base for your spiritual growth journey. Here's what you'll find:

  • Quick Actions: Start a new assessment, discover your spiritual gifts, view progress, or access resources
  • Draft Assessments: Resume any assessments you've started but haven't completed
  • Top Navigation: Access your profile, mentor info, and invitations
3

Taking an Assessment

Complete spiritual health assessments

Assessments help you understand where you are in your spiritual journey and identify areas for growth.

  • Tap "New Assessment" from your dashboard
  • Select an available assessment template
  • Answer each question honestly‚Äîthere are no wrong answers
  • Your progress is auto-saved, so you can pause and continue later
  • When finished, tap "Submit" to complete the assessment
Tip: Take your time with each question. The more honest your answers, the more accurate your results will be.
4

Understanding Your Results

Review your assessment scores and recommendations

After completing an assessment, you'll receive detailed results:

  • Overall Score: Your spiritual health score on a scale
  • Category Breakdown: See how you scored in different areas (prayer, Scripture, service, etc.)
  • Priority Action: One clear next step to focus on
  • Scripture Support: Relevant Bible verses for your growth areas
5

Discovering Your Spiritual Gifts

Take the spiritual gifts assessment

The Spiritual Gifts assessment helps you discover how God has uniquely equipped you to serve.

  • Tap "Spiritual Gifts" from your dashboard
  • Complete the assessment questions
  • View your top gifts ranked by score
  • Read descriptions of each gift and how to use it
Tip: Share your results with your mentor‚Äîthey can help you find ways to serve using your gifts.
6

Connecting with a Mentor

Accept invitations and build your mentorship

Mentors can invite you to connect through the app. Here's how to accept:

  • Check the mail icon in your dashboard for pending invitations
  • Review the mentor's information
  • Tap "Accept" to connect
  • You may need to sign a mentorship agreement

Once connected, your mentor can see your assessment results and help guide your growth journey.

7

Tracking Your Progress

View your growth over time

The Progress section shows how you've grown over time:

  • Tap "View Progress" from your dashboard
  • See your assessment history and score trends
  • Compare results across different time periods
  • Celebrate milestones and improvements
1

Getting Started as a Mentor

Create your account and set up your mentor profile

Download T[root]H Discipleship and create your mentor account:

  • Sign up with your email address or use Google/Apple sign-in
  • Select "Mentor" as your role
  • Complete your profile with your name and information
Tip: Your profile information will be visible to apprentices when you send invitations.
2

Your Mentor Dashboard

Navigate the mentor interface

Your mentor dashboard gives you a complete view of your apprentices and their progress:

  • Apprentices Tab: View all your connected apprentices
  • Assessments Tab: Review submitted assessments
  • Agreements Tab: Manage mentorship agreements
  • Resources Tab: Access mentor resources and guides
  • Quick Stats: See active apprentices, completed assessments, and average scores
3

Inviting Apprentices

Connect with people you want to mentor

To invite someone to become your apprentice:

  • Tap the person+ icon in your dashboard
  • Enter the apprentice's name and email address
  • Tap "Send Invitation"
  • The apprentice will receive an email with instructions to accept
Tip: Follow up with a personal message letting them know to check their email for the invitation.
4

Creating Mentorship Agreements

Establish clear expectations with your apprentices

Mentorship agreements help set expectations for the relationship:

  • Go to the Agreements tab
  • Tap "Send Agreement" for an apprentice
  • The agreement outlines commitments for both parties
  • You sign first, then the apprentice receives it to sign
  • If the apprentice is under 18, a parent signature is also required
5

Reviewing Apprentice Assessments

View and understand your apprentices' results

When an apprentice completes an assessment, you can review their results:

  • Go to the Assessments tab or tap on an apprentice
  • Select a completed assessment to view
  • Review their overall score and category breakdown
  • See their individual answers to each question
  • Read the AI-generated recommendations
  • Add your own mentor notes for future reference
Tip: Use the assessment results as a starting point for your next conversation with your apprentice.
6

Viewing Spiritual Gifts

Help apprentices understand and use their gifts

You can view your apprentices' spiritual gifts results:

  • Tap on an apprentice's profile
  • Navigate to their Spiritual Gifts section
  • See their top gifts and scores
  • Use this information to help them find serving opportunities
7

Using Mentor Resources

Access guides and materials to support your mentoring

The Resources tab provides helpful materials for your mentoring journey:

  • Mentor Guides: Best practices for discipleship
  • Conversation Starters: Questions to ask your apprentices
  • Topic Resources: Materials on specific spiritual topics
  • Weekly Tips: Regular encouragement and ideas
